Component Minimum Specifications Recommended Specifications
Processor 8th Gen Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 Intel Core i5 / AMD Ryzen 5 or better

Storage 256GB SSD 512GB SSD
Display HD minimum HD or better
Operating System Windows 10/11, macOS, or Linux (all supported)

Note: Try bringing a pendrive(>=8GB) for linux installation


Laptop Recommendations:


₹40,000 - ₹60,000:

Suitable for basic needs like coding, assignments, and lectures. May struggle with heavy IDEs or virtual machines.

Recommendations: HP Pavilion, Lenovo Thinkbook 16

₹60,000 - ₹80,000:

Offers a good balance of performance and price. Suitable for all academic and development tasks.

Recommendation: ASUS Vivobook 16, Lenovo Yoga Slim 7, Lenovo Ideapad Slim 5

₹80,000 - ₹1,00,000:

Excellent build and high-end performance. Ideal for students working on large codebases, React, or design tools.

Recommendations: Lenovo IdeaPad Pro 5, ASUS TUF


• The laptops listed above are recommendations based on performance tiers and are not mandatory purchases. Students are encouraged to explore and choose systems that best fit their personal preferences and budget.

• During the induction program, there will be a session to assist students in installing and setting up a Linux-based operating system if not already present.
Mac users will also be guided to ensure their systems are properly configured for the course requirements.
