Letter from Director

Dear Incoming Students


A warm welcome to the IIIT Hyderabad community! We're a tight-knit group dedicated to transforming the world through innovative technology and the skilled professionals we train. As a research-focused institution, we aim to mold future leaders. Over the next two, four, or five years you spend here, we want each of you to develop your own unique worldview – one that encompasses your profession, the society around you, and the wider world. You're now embarking on an exciting journey towards both academic excellence and significant societal impact.

It's perfectly normal to feel a bit anxious, but we encourage you to channel that energy constructively. Dive into the vibrant academic and non-academic environment here. You'll have incredible opportunities to explore the cutting edge of various subjects through your courses, gain deep understanding with hands-on projects, and even push the boundaries of knowledge by engaging in our renowned research. A distinguishing feature of IIIT Hyderabad is the chance for both undergraduate and postgraduate students to get deeply involved in research. We strongly urge you to ignite your curiosity and seize the opportunity to contribute to global knowledge.

At IIIT Hyderabad, we go beyond just teaching "how" to do something. We want you to also critically think about "what" you should do with your skills and "why." This requires deeper study and analysis, moving beyond superficial details. Keep this in mind as you progress through your years here. This institute's reputation has been built by the efforts of its past students, and that mantle now passes to you. We're confident you'll surpass even their achievements, and we eagerly look forward to seeing it unfold!

Dr. P J Narayanan

Professor & Director

IIIT Hyderabad, Hyderabad
