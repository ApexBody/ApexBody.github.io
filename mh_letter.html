Mental Health Support

Welcome to IIIT!


Starting college is a significant milestone, and it can bring a mix of excitement and challenges. We understand that you are away from home and for many this is probably the first time. Whether you're adjusting to a new environment, managing stress, or simply need someone to talk to, we encourage you to reach out to our campus Mental Health Team. Seek help when you need and do not hesitate to reach out.

Our campus Mental Health Team is here to offer support, resources, and guidance to help you thrive academically, socially, and personally at IIIT. We also conduct workshops, talks, and various programs designed to promote mental health and resilience.

Our team comprises of student volunteers, a full-time clinical psychologist, a counsellor, a visiting psychiatrist, an online rehabilitation psychologist and faculty coordinators. Any interaction with any member of the mental health team remains confidential. Our support team provides a welcoming, non-judgmental space for students when they seek help.

Remember, you are not alone — we are here for you every step of the way. Stay connected, prioritize self-care, and don't hesitate to contact us whenever you need support.

Wishing you successful and fulfilling years at IIIT! Continue to chase your dreams and fulfil your ambitions…
