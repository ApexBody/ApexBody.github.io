How to reach IIIT-H

How to make your way to the campus:


Landmarks

1. Gachibowli Stadium
2. ISB (Indian School of Business) Road
3. Opposite to TCS
4. DLF


Airport - Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

Approximately 22 km from IIIT-H

Pushpak Electric Airport Bus directly to Gachibowli (3.5Km from Campus)
Fare: 212/- to 265/- per person
For timings visit: Pushpak Bus Timings

Direct cab from airport to campus
Approximate fare: 750/- to 800/- for a 4-seater cab (subject to change based on time and availability)

Railway Stations

Direct Auto Options:

Secunderabad (Approx 25 km to IIIT-H)
Direct Auto to IIIT - Around ₹350/- to ₹400/-

Nampally (Approx 18 km to IIIT-H)
Direct Auto to IIIT - Approx ₹300/-

Kacheguda (Approx 23 km to IIIT-H)
Direct Auto to IIIT - Approx ₹350/- to ₹400/-

Cherlapally (Approx 38 km to IIIT-H)
Direct Auto to IIIT - Approx ₹450/- to ₹500/-

Metro Option:

Metro services are available from:
• Secunderabad Metro Station
• Nampally Metro Station
to Raidurg Metro Station (6km from campus)
Please check Metro routes online for current schedules and routes.

MMTS Train Option:

From all the above railway stations, you can reach Lingampally station by MMTS (local train).
Lingampally is the closest MMTS station to IIIT (approx 7km)
Auto fare from Lingampally - Approx ₹120/-


Cabs


Uber Cabs [Download mobile app from Playstore]

Buses are also readily available from all over the Hyderabad to IIIT-H at low fares.
(For newcomers to Hyderabad -- please do get it confirmed before boarding if the bus follows the route to IIIT.)
